1. Dua with the Greatest Name (Ismullahi Al-A'zam)

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ بِأَنِّي أَشْهَدُ أَنَّكَ أَنْتَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ الْأَحَدُ الصَّمَدُ الَّذِي لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَهُ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ

Transliteration

Allahumma innee as'aluka bi-annee ash-hadu annaka Anta Allah, la ilaha illa Ant, Al-Ahad As-Samad, al-ladhi lam yalid wa lam yulad, wa lam yakun lahu kufuwan ahad.

Meaning

"O Allah, indeed I ask You by the fact that I bear witness that You are Allah, there is no deity except You, The One, The Eternal (Self-Sufficient Master), Who does not beget and was not born, and there is not for Him any equivalent."

2. The Targeted Dua Against Magicians

Read 3 times
اللَّهُمَّ أَبْطِلْ سِحْرَهُمْ كُلَّهُ، وَاقْطَعْ أَسْبَابَهُمْ جَمِيعًا، وَفَرِّقْ جَمْعَهُمْ، وَشَتِّتْ شَمْلَهُمْ، وَاقْهَرْ شَيَاطِينَهُمْ، وَارْدُدْ كُلَّ بَأْسٍ وَمَكْرٍ وَسِحْرٍ يَصْنَعُونَهُ إِلَيْهِمْ، يَا قَوِيُّ يَا مَتِينُ.

Transliteration

Allahumma abtil sihrahum kullahu, waqta‘ asbabahum jamee‘an, wa farrik jam‘ahum, wa shattit shamlahum, wa aqhir shayateenahum, wardud kulla ba’sin wa makrin wa sihrin yasna‘oonahu ilayhim, ya Qawiyyu ya Matīn.
“O Allah, nullify all of their sihr, cut all their connections, break their unity, scatter them, overpower their devils, and return every harm, plot and sihr they make back upon them — O Mighty, O Firm in Strength.”

3. Repeat the final strike (7x)

اللَّهُمَّ وَارْدُدْ كُلَّ بَأْسٍ وَمَكْرٍ وَسِحْرٍ يَصْنَعُونَهُ إِلَيْهِمْ
Trans: “Ya Allah Return every harm, plot and sihr they produce back upon them.”

4. Reliance on Allah

حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ، وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيمِ
Hasbunallahu wa ni'mal wakeel, wa la hawla wa la quwwata illa billahil 'aliyyil 'adheem.
"Allah is sufficient for us, and He is the best Disposer of affairs. And there is no power nor might except with Allah, the Most High, the Most Great."

Close with

اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ وَسَلِّمْ عَلَى نَبِيِّنَا مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِهِ وَأَصْحَابِهِ أَجْمَعِينَ.
(Allahumma salli wa sallim 'ala nabiyyina Muhammadin wa 'ala alihi wa as-habihi ajma'in.)
