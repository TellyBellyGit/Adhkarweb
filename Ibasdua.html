Ibrahim's (as) Du'a

Supplication from Prophet Ibrahim (Abraham) - Peace be upon him

Return to Menu
1
وَإِذَا مَرِضْتُ فَهُوَ يَشْفِينِ
Wa-itha maridtu fahuwa yashfeeni
"And when I am ill, it is He who cures me."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:80)
2
وَالَّذِي يُمِيتُنِي ثُمَّ يُحْيِينِ
Wal-ladhi yumituni thumma yuhyeeni
"And He who will cause me to die and then bring me to life."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:81)
3
وَالَّذِي أَطْمَعُ أَنْ يَغْفِرَ لِي خَطِيئَتِي يَوْمَ الدِّينِ
Wal-ladhi atma'u an yaghfira li khati'ati yawma ad-deen
"And He who I hope will forgive me my sin on the Day of Recompense."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:82)
4
رَبِّ هَبْ لِي حُكْمًا وَأَلْحِقْنِي بِالصَّالِحِينَ
Rabbi hab li hukman walhiqni bis-saliheen
"My Lord, grant me judgment and join me with the righteous."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:83)
5
وَجَعَلْ لِي لِسَانَ صِدْقٍ فِي الْآخِرِينَ
Wajil li lisanan sidqin fil-akhireen
"And grant me a reputation of honor among the later generations."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:84)
6
وَجَعَلْنِي مِنْ وَرَثَةِ جَنَّةِ النَّعِيمِ
Wajil ni min warathati jannati an-na'eem
"And place me among the inheritors of the Garden of Delight."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:85)
7
وَلَا تُخْزِنِي يَوْمَ يُبْعَثُونَ
Wala tukhzeenee yawma yub'athoon
"And do not disgrace me on the Day they are resurrected."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:87)
8
يَوْمَ لَا يَنفَعُ مَالٌ وَلَا بَنُونَ
Yawma la yanfa'u malun wala banoon
"The Day when there will be no benefit in wealth or children."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:88)
9
إِلَّا مَنْ أَتَى اللَّهَ بِقَلْبٍ سَلِيمٍ
Illa man atallaha biqalbin saleem
"Except one who comes to Allah with a sound heart."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:89)

Islamic Supplications. May Allah accept our du'as.
