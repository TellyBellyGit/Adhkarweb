Dua for Cure

Islamic Healing Supplications and Ruqyah

1
Prayer for Healing

اللَّهُمَّ رَبَّ النَّاسِ، أَذْهِبِ الْبَأْسَ، اشْفِ أَنْتَ الشَّافِي، لاَ شِفَاءَ إِلاَّ شِفَاؤُكَ، شِفَاءً لاَ يُغَادِرُ سَقَمًا

Allāhumma Rabban-nās, adhhib al-ba's, ishfi anta ash-Shāfī, lā shifā'a illā shifā'uka, shifā'an lā yughadiru saqaman
"O Allah, Lord of mankind, remove the harm and cure (him/her). You are the Curer. There is no cure except Your cure, a cure that leaves no illness behind."
Bukhārī 5743, Muslim 2191
Said when visiting the sick.
2
Seeking Refuge from Pain

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ (ثَلاثًا). أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ وَقُدْرَتِهِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا أَجِدُ وَأُحَاذِرُ (سَبْعَ مَرَّاتٍ)

Bismillāh (3x). A'ūdhu billāhi wa qudratihi min sharri mā ajidu wa uḥādhir (7x)
"In the Name of Allah (3x). I seek refuge in Allah and His Power from the evil of what I feel and what I fear (7x)."
Muslim 2202
Place hand on area of pain, say 3x "Bismillah," then 7x the rest.
3
Ruqyah by Jibrīl

بِاسْمِ اللَّهِ أَرْقِيكَ، مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ يُؤْذِيكَ، مِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ نَفْسٍ أَوْ عَيْنٍ حَاسِدٍ، اللَّهُ يَشْفِيكَ، بِاسْمِ اللَّهِ أَرْقِيكَ

Bismillāhi arqīka, min kulli shay'in yu'dhīka, min sharri kulli nafsin aw ʿaynin ḥāsid, Allāhu yashfīka, bismillāhi arqīka
"In the Name of Allah, I perform ruqyah for you, from everything that is harming you, from the evil of every soul or envious eye. May Allah cure you. In the Name of Allah, I perform ruqyah for you."
Muslim 2186
Recited by Jibrīl (ʿalayhi as-salām) over the Prophet ﷺ when ill.
4
Asking Allah for Cure

أَسْأَلُ اللَّهَ الْعَظِيمَ رَبَّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ أَنْ يَشْفِيَكَ

As'alu Allāh al-ʿAẓīm Rabb al-ʿArsh al-ʿAẓīm an yashfīka
"I ask Allah the Magnificent, Lord of the Magnificent Throne, to cure you."
Abū Dāwūd 3106, Tirmidhī 2083, Ibn Mājah 1441 (ḥasan)
Say this 7 times when visiting the sick.
