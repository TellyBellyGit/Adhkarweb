Protection Verses

Islamic Supplications for Protection and Refuge

1
Seeking Refuge from Satan

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ

Aʿūdhu billāhi min ash-shayṭān ir-rajīm
(I seek refuge with Allah from the accursed Shayṭān)
2
Command to Leave

اخْرُجْ بِسْمِ اللَّهِ

Ukhruj bismillāh
(Leave, in the Name of Allah)
3
Disgracing the Enemy

اخْسَأْ يَا عَدُوَّ اللَّهِ

Ikhsa' yā ʿaduww Allāh
(Be disgraced, O enemy of Allah)
4
Truth vs Falsehood (17:81)

قَدْ جَاءَ الْحَقُّ وَزَهَقَ الْبَاطِلُ ۚ إِنَّ الْبَاطِلَ كَانَ زَهُوقًا

Qad jāʾa l-ḥaqqu wa zahqa l-bāṭil, inna l-bāṭila kāna zahūqā
(Truth has come, and falsehood has perished; surely falsehood is bound to perish)
5
Seeking Refuge and Cursing

أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنْكَ، أَلْعَنُكَ بِلَعْنَةِ اللَّهِ

Aʿūdhu billāhi minka, alʿanuka bilaʿnatillāh
(I seek refuge in Allah from you; I curse you with the curse of Allah)
6
No Power Except with Allah

لَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيمِ

Lā quwwata illā billāh al-ʿAliyy al-ʿAẓīm
(There is no power except with Allah, the Most High, the Magnificent)
7
Allah's Party is Victorious (5:56)

فَإِنَّ حِزْبَ اللَّهِ هُمُ الْغَالِبُونَ

Fa-inna ḥizballāhi humu l-ghālibūn
(Indeed, the party of Allah — they are the victorious)
8
Allah is Sufficient

حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ

Ḥasbiyallāhu wa niʿma l-wakīl
(Allah is sufficient for me, and the best Disposer of affairs)
