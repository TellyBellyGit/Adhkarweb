Quran Verses

Selected verses from the Holy Quran

1
لَا تَحْزَنْ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ مَعَنَا ۖ فَأَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ سَكِينَتَهُۥ عَلَيْهِ وَأَيَّدَهُۥ بِجُنُودٍۢ لَّمْ تَرَوْهَا وَجَعَلَ كَلِمَةَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ ٱلسُّفْلَىٰ ۗ وَكَلِمَةُ ٱللَّهِ هِىَ ٱلْعُلْيَا ۗ وَٱللَّهُ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ ٤٠
Laa tahzan inna Allaaha ma'anaa fa-anzala Allaahu sakeenatahu 'alayhi wa ayyadahu bi-junoodin lam tarawhaa wa ja'ala kalimata alladheena kafaroo as-suflaa wa kalimatu Allaahi hiya al-'ulyaa wa Allaahu 'azeezun hakeem
"[He said] to his companion, 'Do not grieve; indeed Allah is with us.' And Allah sent down his tranquillity upon him and supported him with angels you did not see and made the word of those who disbelieved the lowest, while the word of Allah - that is the highest. And Allah is Exalted in Might and Wise."
Source: Surah At-Tawbah (9:40)
2
وَمَا تَكُونُ فِي شَأْنٍ وَمَا تَتْلُو مِنْهُ مِنْ قُرْآنٍ وَلَا تَعْمَلُونَ مِنْ عَمَلٍ إِلَّا كُنَّا عَلَيْكُمْ شُهُودًا إِذْ تُفِيضُونَ فِيهِ ۚ وَمَا يَعْزُبُ عَنْ رَبِّكَ مِنْ مِثْقَالِ ذَرَّةٍ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَلَا أَصْغَرَ مِنْ ذَٰلِكَ وَلَا أَكْبَرَ إِلَّا فِي كِتَابٍ مُبِينٍ
Wa maa takoonu fee sha'ninw wa maa tatloo minhu min qur'aaninw wa laa ta'maloona min 'amalin illaa kunnaa 'alaikum shuhoodan iz tufeedoona feeh; wa maa ya'zubu 'ar Rabbika min misqaali zarratin fil ardi wa laa fis samaaa'i wa laaa asghara min zaalika wa laaa akbara illaa fee Kitaabin Mubeen
"There is no activity you may be engaged in ˹O Prophet˺ or portion of the Quran you may be reciting, nor any deed you ˹all˺ may be doing except that We are a Witness over you while doing it. Not ˹even˺ an atom's weight is hidden from your Lord on earth or in heaven; nor anything smaller or larger than that, but is ˹written˺ in a perfect Record."
Source: Surah Yunus (10:61)
3
وَالَّذِي أَطْمَعُ أَنْ يَغْفِرَ لِي خَطِيئَتِي يَوْمَ الدِّينِ
Wal-ladhi atma'u an yaghfira li khati'ati yawma ad-deen
"And He who I hope will forgive me my sin on the Day of Recompense."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:82)
4
رَبِّ هَبْ لِي حُكْمًا وَأَلْحِقْنِي بِالصَّالِحِينَ
Rabbi hab li hukman walhiqni bis-saliheen
"My Lord, grant me judgment and join me with the righteous."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:83)
5
وَجَعَلْ لِي لِسَانَ صِدْقٍ فِي الْآخِرِينَ
Wajil li lisanan sidqin fil-akhireen
"And grant me a reputation of honor among the later generations."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:84)
6
وَجَعَلْنِي مِنْ وَرَثَةِ جَنَّةِ النَّعِيمِ
Wajil ni min warathati jannati an-na'eem
"And place me among the inheritors of the Garden of Delight."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:85)
7
وَلَا تُخْزِنِي يَوْمَ يُبْعَثُونَ
Wala tukhzeenee yawma yub'athoon
"And do not disgrace me on the Day they are resurrected."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:87)
8
يَوْمَ لَا يَنفَعُ مَالٌ وَلَا بَنُونَ
Yawma la yanfa'u malun wala banoon
"The Day when there will be no benefit in wealth or children."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:88)
9
إِلَّا مَنْ أَتَى اللَّهَ بِقَلْبٍ سَلِيمٍ
Illa man atallaha biqalbin saleem
"Except one who comes to Allah with a sound heart."
Source: Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:89)

May Allah (swt) accept our du'as.
